Realms:
    >> Runic Flesh
        Runes are drawn into the flesh, empowering the body
            - Strength Runes -> Runic Strength Skill
            - Vitality Runes -> Runic Vitality Skill
            - Endurance Runes
            - Armour Runes -> Runic Armor Skill

    >> Runic Soul
        Runes are drawn into the Soul, empowering the soul
